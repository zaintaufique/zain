About

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Turku working on efficient execution of AI workloads on heterogeneous computing systems. My research focuses on runtime systems, multi-DNN scheduling, and optimization of edge AI inference across CPU, GPU and NPU platforms.

My work studies techniques for improving latency, throughput and energy efficiency of modern AI systems deployed on edge devices.

Research Interests

  • Edge AI systems
  • AI inference optimization
  • heterogeneous computing
  • runtime systems for machine learning
  • embedded AI platforms
